  SLAE Exam

        September 3, 2020

Good afternoon Tim, I am starting this blog series because I'm attempting the SecurityTube Linux Assembly Expert (SLAE) exam in order to return to my low-level roots. This material also serves as a stepping stone to Offensive Security Certified Practitioner (OSCP). Both exams heavily stress documenting results and there have been OSCP students who failed due to poor documentation in otherwise great work; therefore, copious note taking is strongly recommended during these exams. Fortunately there is no time constraint for the SLAE exam unlike OSCP.
